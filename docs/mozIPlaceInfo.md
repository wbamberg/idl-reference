### placeId ###

The machine-local (internal) id of the place.  
  
</pre>
### guid ###
<pre>

</pre>
### uri ###
<pre>

</pre>

<pre>  
The title associated with the place.  
  
</pre>
### frecency ###
<pre>

</pre>
